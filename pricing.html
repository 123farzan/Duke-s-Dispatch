— PRICING FAQ

What operators ask about the fee.

What does the fixed weekly fee mean?

It means the same dollar amount every week, regardless of how many loads you run, how much you gross, or what equipment you're in. If you run one load or ten, you pay the same fixed weekly fee. No percentage of your revenue, no commission, no surprise add-ons.

What does the weekly revenue share plan look like?

That plan is tied to the revenue your truck generates each week. We support the same dispatch work, but the fee is a percentage of the weekly revenue instead of a fixed weekly payment.

Do I have to sign a contract?

No. We work week-to-week. You can quit any Tuesday and not owe anything for the following week. We earn your business every week.

What if I'm a team operation with multiple trucks?

We discount per-truck for fleets of 3+. Talk to us — we'll build a custom plan with hybrid pricing that usually beats our flat fee at scale.

When does the week start?

Monday 00:00 to Sunday 23:59 CT. We bill on the following Monday. If you start mid-week, we prorate the first week to the day.

What payment methods do you accept?

ACH direct debit (preferred), wire, or factoring-company deduction. Credit cards accepted but with a 3% processing fee.
